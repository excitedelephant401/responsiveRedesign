Current Issues

Usability Problems

  • Legibility issues
    1. Links are in a light color
    2. There isn't separation between ways to help the organization
  • Lack of minimalist design
    1. Repetitive information on the website where multiple bullet points have the same information
    2. Lack of detailed information. Many related concepts are scattered on the website on many pages.
    3. Email address repeats on the site
    4. Contact information takes up a lot of space in the footer
  • Inefficiencies of use
    1. The only action users can take are link redirects
  • Inconsistency
    1. The white section above the footer has information that doesn't align with the rest of the page
  • Errors
    1. The link to the interest form redirects to a faulty URL

Accessibility Issues

  1. There is little contrast between the background and link color. WebAIM WAVE located this issue and I agree that it's hard to read the links.
  2. There are noscript elements on the page. I wouldn't have noticed this without WebAIM but agree it's an issue because many people use JavaScript.
